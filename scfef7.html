Security Alert

Dear customer,

Our Technology partner “Trusteer”, who allows us to protect your transaction online and prevent identity theft, has recently been a victim of phishing email scam.

The e-mail scam appears to come from support@trusteer.com.

The content of the message requests that recipients run an attached file in order to upgrade their IBM Trusteer Rapport software.

Please note that IBM Trusteer will not distribute Rapport updates as email attachments.

A new update is being rolled out to protect and prevent users from accidentally opening the attached malicious file.

The below actions are recommended to avoid being a victim of this scam:

1.Users who received this email are requested to delete it immediately.

2.Users who may have already launched the attached file should stop using their online banking and contact Ecobank Support.

Yours Sincerely,

Ecobank, the Pan-African Bank
