Premier Banking
When you enjoy the finer things in life, you want a bank that gives you more. A bank that’s tailored to you.
As a high net worth individual, Premier Banking provides a Relationship Manager for 24/7 financial and banking advice. You’ll also enjoy a variety of exclusive lifestyle benefits, including preferential airline and hotel rates, and rewards from luxury brands. You will also have the added assurance of purchase protection, and concierge services.
Premier Banking offers you a choice of accounts and services to suit your needs. Here’s a selection:
Premier Banking Current Account
A multi-currency current account with a minimum account balance and interest rate paid on balances *
Premier Banking Savings Account
A multi-currency savings account designed to make your money work harder
Premier Banking Investment Account
For investments in fixed deposits, treasury bills, banker’s acceptance and commercial papers
Asset Management and Trustee Services
Portfolio management services, investment advisory services and stock brokerage services
Ecobank Premier Advance Account
Provides a cash advance of 50% of your net monthly income and an Ecobank Platinum Debit Card
Ecobank Mortgages
Premier Banking customers can benefit from preferential interest rates for mortgages. Ecobank Mortgages for Premier customers are available for financing and refinancing of existing properties.
Premier Personal Loans
Loan facility delivered quickly, amount varying in each country
Priority Pass
This gives you access to airport lounges in over 600 airports around the world
Ecobank Platinum Card
Gives you 24/7 access to your funds in accounts, both locally and internationally
e-banking products
Keep on top of your financial affairs with email, e-statements alerts and SMS messages
Internet Banking
Safe and secure real time banking with 24/7 access
Rapidtransfer
Transfer of funds quickly and easily both in Nigeria and across Africa
International Money Transfer
Transfer and receive funds internationally with Western Union, MoneyGram and RIA
International Partnerships
Enjoy exceptional offers brought to you by Shangri-La Hotels and Resorts, in over 85 locations worldwide (please ask your Relationship Manager for your personal discount code)
Access to Premier lounges
A private and exclusive environment in which to conduct your banking or financial transactions. These offer fully equipped business areas, including meeting rooms and small boardrooms, and dedicated areas to catch up on the latest business and finance news
Invitations to lifestyle events
Invitations to sponsored events
