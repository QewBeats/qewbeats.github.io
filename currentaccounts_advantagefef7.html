Advantage Banking
As a salaried employee or as a self-employed individual Ecobank has created a way of banking that’s designed to meet your modern lifestyle. Together, we’ll manage and grow your wealth.
As an Advantage client, you’ll enjoy a dedicated Relationship Manager to help you with day-to-day banking needs, and round the clock banking with a free multilingual Customer Contact Centre.
You’ll also have a choice of accounts and services to suit your needs. Here’s a selection:
Salary Current Account
Everything you need for your day-to-day banking needs
Personal Loan
If you work in private and public employment this all-purpose loan will help you manage your lifestyle
Salary Advance and
Ecobank Advance Account
Both these accounts work as overdrafts on your salary so you’ll have extra cash when you need it.
Asset Acquisition
Help to buy cars, generators and other important purchases
Ecobank Mortgages
Choose from a variety of financing to help with purchasing a home, construction, renovation and home refinance.
Advantage Flexi Account
This hybrid savings account has all the features of a current account
Savings Domiciliary Account
This is a savings account maintained in different foreign currencies
Save As You Spend Account
An account that warehouses and saves 1–10% of what you spend every time you shop
Term Deposit
With this account, a fixed amount in local or foreign currency is locked in for a fixed period time
Ecobank Gold Card
Gives you 24/7 access to your funds, both locally and internationally
Alerts and e-Statements
Keep on top of your financial affairs with email and e-statements alerts
Text Banking
Check your balance and get a mini statement by text message
Internet Banking
Safe and secure real time banking with 24/7 access
Rapidtransfer
Transfer of funds quickly and easily both in country and across Africa
International Money Transfer
Transfer and receive funds internationally with Western Union, MoneyGram and RIA *
Comprehensive Insurance
Enjoy special concessionary rates *
Plus, you can also enjoy:
- Advantage lounges with interactive facilities all over the country
- Fast track in-branch services
- Loyalty schemes
* varies according to country
