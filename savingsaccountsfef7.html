Ecobank Savings Account
The secure, convenient and reliable savings account.
We want you to get the most out of your money and save for the future. The best way to do this is to open an Ecobank Savings Account. Your ESA comes with an Ecobank debit card, which gives you access to our network of over 4,000 ATM’s and POS terminals across Africa.

Take advantage of Ecobank savings account along with the following features:
  • Debit card
  • Internet Banking
  • Mobile Banking
  • SMS-Alert
  • e-Alert
  • Monthly e-Statement
Additonal options:
  • Current account (if eligible)
  • Standing order
