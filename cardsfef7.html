Do I need to have an account with Ecobank in order to obtain a card?

No. The Ecobank cards provide both the Ecobank account holder and non-account holder with a full range of banking solutions. Once you apply for an Ecobank card we will open an account for you. Customers requiring the prepaid cards in the form of gift, payroll and travel cards will have a virtual card account and do not need to open an account.

 
Frequently Asked Questions
Q: What is an Ecobank Debit Card? What is an Ecobank Debit Card?
  A: An Ecobank Debit Card is a card that gives you direct access to your Bank Account for cash withdrawals and purchases at Merchant locations within your home country and abroad.
Q: How do I obtain an Ecobank Debit Card? How do I obtain an Ecobank Debit Card?
  A: • Open a Current*/Savings Account in any of our branches and request for a card.
• If you are already an Ecobank customer, complete an eProducts application form and submit at the nearest Ecobank branch. Note:* Conditions apply
Q: Who qualifies for an ECOBANK Card? Who qualifies for an ECOBANK Card?
  A: Every customer of Ecobank qualifies to hold an Ecobank debit card
Q: Can I use my Ecobank Debit Card on the Internet? Can I use my Ecobank Debit Card on the Internet?
  A: Yes, if it is internet enabled like the Ecobank Visa Classic Card, Gold Credit Card, MasterCard etc.
Q: How do I use my Ecobank Card on the ATM? How do I use my Ecobank Card on the ATM?
  A: I. Please insert your card into the marked slot on the ATM.
II. When the screen appears, use the keypad to enter the 4-digit Personal Identification Number (PIN), which was provided with the card at the time of issuance. Please note that you have to press “Proceed” on the ATM after entering your PIN code.
III. After the ATM accepts your PIN, select the transaction that you want to do and press the “Proceed” button to continue with the transaction. For e.g. to do Cash Withdrawal Transaction, follow the steps below.

Once the ATM accepts your PIN, a screen with all the types of transactions available on the ATM will appear.
a. Select Cash Withdrawal from the Menu
b. Select the account you want to withdraw from, whether current or Savings
c. Various amounts will be displayed on the screen, select your required amount
d. If you require another amount, select “Other” on the screen and using the keypad, enter the amount you wish to withdraw. Select “Proceed” to continue
e. If you want a receipt, select “Yes”, otherwise select “No” from the menu when the machine prompts you
f. Please take your card from the slot
g. Then take your cash from the cash slot
h. If you requested for a receipt, kindly wait and take your receipt from the receipt slot.


Balance Inquiry
For balance inquiry, please go through the PIN verification.
a. Once PIN has been verified, select balance inquiry from main menu
b. Select Account on which you would like to do the transaction
c. Once account has been selected, punch the “Proceed” button
Mini Statement
To print the last few transactions done on your account, please go through the PIN verification process.
a. Select Mini Statement from the main menu
b. Select the desired account
c. Punch “Proceed” from the screen
