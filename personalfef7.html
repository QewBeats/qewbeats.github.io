Welcome to Personal Banking
If you’re looking for more from your bank than a standard current or savings account, we are delighted to offer a choice of Personal Banking products.
As a salaried employee or as a self employed individual, Advantage Banking is designed to suit the needs of you and your family. While high net worth customers are invited to join Premier Banking and let us provide the perfect financial solutions tailored specially for you.
Addvantage Banking
As an Advantage customer, you'll have access to both everyday banking and credit to meet your needs. Designed for salaried employees and self-employed individuals, we've created a way of banking that suits busy life. A Relationship Manager will work you to manage and grow your wealth with carefully considered savings and investment products. You'll also receive an Ecobank Gold Card.
Find out about banking with Ecobank Advantage >
Premier Banking
As a Premier Customer, you'll enjoy a service that's specifically tailored to you. A dedicated Relationship Manager will look after your every need and you'll have access to exclusive products, services and rewards. Premier banking offers you a range of multi-currency saving and lending products with preferential rates, as well as asset management and trustee services. You'll also receive an Ecobank Platinum Card.
