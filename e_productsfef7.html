e-Products
We are proud to offer a range of individual accounts, tailored to suit all our customers' financial and banking needs.
Already have an Ecobank Account? Subscribe to an Ecobank card today and enjoy SMS-Alerts, e-Alerts, e-Statements, Standing Orders and Internet Banking. Download and complete an e-Products form and Deed of indemnity form below and return both to your nearest Ecobank branch.

e-Statements : less paper, more flexibility!

e-Statements via email ensure that you are fully appraised of activities on your account over periods that you determine.

e-Alerts

Ecobank’s e-Alerts offer an early warning service, via SMS and email, of debits and credits on your account due to: cash & cheque withdrawals and deposits; debit card transactions at ATMs; funds transfers; and any other transaction that may affect your account balance. We will require your mobile phone number (SMS alerts) and/or email address and accounts from which you would like to receive eAlerts.

SMS Banking

Our SMS banking service allows you access to your Ecobank account wherever you are. You can request your account balance or your statement, and view the last three transactions on your account through SMS. Signing up is as simple as providing your mobile phone number and account number.
