Ecobank Current Account
Value added accounts available in both local and foreign currency

The Ecobank Current Account opens doors to a whole new world of banking opportunities! As an Ecobank account holder, you can enjoy many privileges. Ecobank Current Account comes with the following features:

  • Cheques services
  • Linked Savings Account
  • Standing Order
  • Internet banking
 
  • SMS-Alert
  • e-Alert
  • e-Statement - monthly
  • Debits Cards - Ecobank Regional or Visa/Mastercard where available

You will enjoy fully networked and efficient banking services with 24/7 access to your funds in over 1305 branches across 36 countries in Africa.

New Account Opening
To open an Ecobank Current Account, download the relevant account opening form below, along with a Deed of Indemnity, and return the completed forms to your nearest Ecobank branch.

Individual Account Opening Form
for an individual account or sole proprietorship
Joint Account Opening Form
if you wish to share an account with your partner, or a minor
Individual Account Opening Form (Nigeria)
for an individual account or sole proprietorship in Nigeria only
Joint Account Opening Form (Nigeria)
if you want to open a joint, partnership and minor account in Nigeria only
Deed of Indemnity Form (Compulsory)
to allow electronic instructions and it is compulsory.

e-Banking
Already have an Ecobank Current Account? Subscribe to an Ecobank card today and enjoy FREE SMS-Alerts, e-Alerts, e-Statements, Standing Orders and Internet Banking. Download and complete an e-Products and Deed of Indemnity form below and return it to your nearest Ecobank branch.
Alternatively, apply at any Ecobank branch to open your account or Contact Us.
