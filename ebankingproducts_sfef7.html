Internet Banking
Secure, efficient cash management and information at your fingertips.

Efficient cash management is an increasingly important factor in business today. Information at your finger tips on your account position is crucial for decision-making.

Enjoy online, easy, real-time access to all your account information in the comfort of your home, your office or from any remote location:

  • Online and real-time account information
  • Account Statements downloadable in Excel, MS-Money and PDF formats
  • Account history
  • Term Deposits
  • Loan/Standing Instruction EnquirIes
  • Balance enquiry
  • Transaction details
  • A window on all your accounts with Ecobank across Africa

Our internet banking solution covers the Ecobank network in 18 countries in West and Central Africa, making it possible for Corporates operating in more than one country in the regions to access their various accounts at any Ecobank affiliate, irrespective of country or currency. Ecobank Internet Banking is available in both French and English.

As the security of customers is of paramount importance to Ecobank, access to Ecobank Internet Banking is via a key encryption code and a password which is changed every two months. This ensures that only authorized users have access to the system.

Ecobank Internet Banking team will provide training and support on this service. A user guide will also be provided for quick reference.
